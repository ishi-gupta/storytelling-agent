Genre: Science Fiction / Time Travel
Place: San Francisco Bay Area, 1969
Time: Summer 1969, alternate timeline branches
Theme: Unintended consequences, technological determinism, the weight of knowledge
Tone: Thoughtful with moments of humor, tinged with melancholy
Point of View: Third person limited, following Dr. Maya Chen
Characters: Dr. Maya Chen (physicist, 35), Douglas Engelbart (inventor), Karen (Maya's colleague from 2025)
Premise: Maya Chen time-travels to witness the first computer mouse demo but accidentally causes a fire that destroys Engelbart's lab, erasing decades of foundational computing work and preventing the internet's invention.
